About Our University

Alatoo University

The state governing body of the university is the Ministry of Education and Science of the Kyrgyz Republic.

The founder of Ala-Too International University is Sapat International Educational Institutions.

Ala-Too International University (AIU) was established in 1996 and it is located in Bishkek, the Kyrgyz Republic.

AIU is a legal entity, carries out its activities in accordance with the legislation of the Kyrgyz Republic.
